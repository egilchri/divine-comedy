Purgatorio * Canto VIII

As evening falls in the Valley of the Rulers, a soul initiates the singing of the hymn "Te lucis ante terminum," which the other spirits join. Two angels, dressed in green and carrying flaming swords with broken tips, descend from the sky and take up guard on opposite sides of the valley. Sordello explains they are protecting the valley from a serpent. Dante and Virgil descend to converse with the souls. Dante meets a spirit who identifies himself as Nino Visconti. Surprised to find Dante alive, Nino asks him to tell his daughter, Giovanna, to pray for him. Later, Sordello points out a serpent slithering through the grass. The two angels fly down, forcing the serpent to flee before they return to their posts. Following this event, another soul, Corrado Malaspina, speaks with Dante, asking for news of his family’s lands. Dante praises the Malaspina family’s honor, and Corrado responds with a prophecy that Dante will soon experience their hospitality firsthand.
